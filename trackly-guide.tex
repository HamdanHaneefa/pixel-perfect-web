\section{Tips for Staying Consistent}

\vspace{2pt}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=3pt, topsep=0pt,
  label=\textbf{\textcolor{accent}{\arabic*.}}]
  \item \textbf{Start with 3--5 habits.}
    {\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
    Too many at once makes it hard to stay consistent. Build the tracking habit first.}

  \item \textbf{Check in at the same time daily.}
    {\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
    Attach it to an existing routine --- morning coffee, before bed, after lunch.}

  \item \textbf{Don't break the chain.}
    {\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
    Once you see a streak in the monthly view, you won't want to break it. Use that.}

  \item \textbf{Review weekly.}
    {\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
    Spend 2 minutes every Sunday in the weekly view. Plan the next week from there.}

  \item \textbf{Be honest.}
    {\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
    Only check off habits you actually completed. Accurate data is the only useful data.}
\end{enumerate}

\section{Frequently Asked Questions}

\vspace{2pt}

\noindent\textbf{I see ``Access Required'' after signing in.}\\
{\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
You signed in with the wrong Google account. Sign out and sign back in using the account that matches your checkout email.}

\vspace{5pt}

\noindent\textbf{Can I use Trackly on my phone?}\\
{\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
Yes. Open \href{https://trackly.elamai.in}{trackly.elamai.in} in Chrome or Safari on your phone. Fully optimised for mobile. You can also add it to your home screen.}

\vspace{5pt}

\noindent\textbf{Will I lose data if I clear my browser?}\\
{\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
No. Your data is stored securely in the cloud, linked to your account. Access it from any device at any time.}

\vspace{5pt}

\noindent\textbf{How do I add or remove habits?}\\
{\fontsize{8.5}{11}\selectfont\color{muted}
Inside the app, use the habit management section to add, rename, or remove habits. Changes take effect immediately.}
